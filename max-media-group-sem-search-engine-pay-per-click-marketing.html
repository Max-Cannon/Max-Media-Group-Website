Keyword Relevance

It is critical that the keywords you select are relevant to your company and it's offerings. Nothing discourages a visitor more than being sent to a site that has nothing to do with his search. Even if the visitor leaves immediately, you've still paid for the click.

The landing page for each PPC ad should be highly relevant to the search being performed. We selects relevant keywords designed to direct those visitors to your website who are most likely to be interested in your products and services.

Geographical Targeting

If you are only looking to attract local customers, you can specify that your ad run only on results geared to your neighborhood, your city or your state (i.e. - Orlando Dentist).

5-Step Paid Media Advertising Process

Research & Discovery

We learn about the client’s business, website, and competitors in order to provide clear documentation to be used throughout all other stages of the project.

*Discovery Report
Define client information & business goals along with all required information to properly complete discovery process
* Paid Media Audit
Create a baseline for current paid media performance based on analytics, landing pages, and current campaign statistics
* Keyword Research
Define target keywords for AdWords, or other PPC campaigns based on business goals outlined in the project brief and identify opportunities from the paid media audit
* Competitive Analysis
Define PPC competitors and ad copy based on keyword research and determine areas of opportunity and risk

Strategy In Advertising

Create a comprehensive paid media strategy and project plan that clearly outlines deliverables and measurable business goals.

* Paid Media Strategy
Define all paid search, display, remarketing, PPC advertising, and social media advertising campaigns, including landing page recommendations, ad copy, targeting options, and budget
* Measurement Planning
Document measureable short-term and long-term goals to evaluate campaign performance.

Campaign Landing Page Setup

Implement landing page recommendations from the paid media strategy with the development team or collaboration with the client

* Content Strategies
Adjust website copy based on a cohesive content strategy to reflect targeted keywords found during the keyword research phase
* Technical Strategies
Implement edits to the website’s coding based on the strategy recommendations. This could include changes to landing page elements such as meta descriptions, internal links, or anything that would help support the overarching objective of the campaigns

Monitor & Optimize

Monitor and adjust campaigns based on the performance of keywords, landing pages, and ad copy

* Short-Term Adjustments
Change bids, ads, and landing pages based on short term performance indicators (daily or every few days)
* Campaign Adjustments
Make global changes to the campaign based on trends that are identified over time (weekly or biweekly)

Analysis & Reporting

Provide regular reporting that includes analysis of campaign performance, KPIs, and goal tracking and adjust project plan based on results

*Monthly Performance Reports
Define client information & business goals along with all required information to properly complete discovery process
* Quarterly Performance Reviews
Comprehensive overview of campaign and adjustments to the project plan based on the results achieved

4 Keys of a Successful 
Paid Media Campaign

Our Selection Of Proper Keywords: The keywords you target (and pay for) must be specific to what you are offering on your website. If you are a retailer, we make sure that people who are looking for the products you sell are funneled directly to an e-commerce landing page where that purchase can be facilitated.

Landing Page Creation & Optimization

Each landing page should be tailored to your unique products or services, and optimized for the keywords you’re targeting in your paid media efforts. With each ad campaign that you drive visitors to your site with, each landing page should be relevant to the content in that campaign.

Continual Reporting & Analysis

Whether you’re running a paid search campaign, or a social media advertising campaign, it’s essential that the proper reporting is completed. This way, we can identify what works best for driving conversions and reaching your goals, and optimize your campaigns based on those findings on a regular basis.

Inbound Link & External Content Building

Monthly link building and content outreach takes place in order to augment ranking growth. We utilize relavant, high-value external blogs,  local listings and social media websites to provide effective inbound links, to supplement paid media linking.

Paid Media Target Enhancement

Another great advantage of Pay Per Click advertising is the flexibility it offers in terms of delivery. There are a number of PPC techniques you can use to create an audience that is most likely to be your target audience. Our pay-per-click marketing strategies are designed for our clients to experience an increase in qualified traffic while also seeing higher conversion rates.
