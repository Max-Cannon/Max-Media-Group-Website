What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of increasing the visibility of a website in organic (un-paid) search engine results position (SERP). There are a number of ways to optimize your website, such as building your site using elements that are easily read by search engines. However, the real 'secret sauce' to a high Search Engine Ranking Position (SERP) for specific keyword phrases is to solve the problems of your target audience and by answering their questions with relevant content...very simple stuff.


What kind of solutions/answers to problems/questions is your target audience of consumers looking for? SEO is all about establishing your company as a leader and authoritative figure in your particular industry. Ask yourself: Does your website contain quality content that not only provides solutions/answers to the problems/questions that your consumers are asking, but is also relevant enough that they want to share it online? If you answered yes, then driving targeted traffic to your site will be a breeze. 


At Max Media Group, our comprehensive SEO services help drive traffic to your website by improving keyword rankings in search engines. For instance, if you searched, “Orlando SEO Services” to find this page, we’ll make sure we provide the solutions/answers to your search query. Establishing your website as a topical authority makes your company a highly reputable source in the eyes of your target-market pool of consumers. Search engines can easily tell if you are delighting your website visitors by how long they remain on your website and how many web pages they visit while there. The more relevant your vistors find your website content, the higher ranking position you will have in the search engine results for specific key-word searches.


Why Is Search Engine Optimization (SEO) So Important?

Your company could have the most beautiful website on the Internet, but if no one knows it’s there or if users don’t actively engage with it once they visit, you might as well not have one at all. One of the key factors when it comes to building your website is thinking like your target audience.


If you have a question about a product or service, how do you look for the answer? You type it into Google, and check to see what comes up. When your website is constantly creating fresh, relevant, and most importantly, search engine optimized content and answers the questions that people are asking. They’ll likely come back to your site the next time they’re looking for more information. Focusing on SEO, will generate more traffic to your site. Convert first-time site visitors to leads and build your brand’s recognition as an industry authority.


It is important to always think about your target audience when creating a well navigated website. The objective is to create a path on your website that, essentially, answers any question they may have. However, your target audience isn’t the only one who needs to easily navigate. Search engines have what is called web crawlers. They travel through the Internet from website to website, indexing content as they go. SEO works to properly code your website in a way that’s easy for web crawlers to navigate. You want the crawlers to index your website in order to show up higher on the Google and Bing SERPs.
