About Max Media Group

By Max Cannon, CEO & CTO posted June 20, 2017

  OUR SIMPLE PHILOSOPHY.  We believe that today, and every day, we must deliver more than we are getting paid to do. Meaning, we believe the victory of success will truly be won when daily providing our clients with more than is expected in each task we perform on their behalf.

OUR LONG HISTORY.

Max Media Group was born March 15th, 1995, in Seattle, Washington, in close conjunction with the birth of the modern internet and newly budding entities such as Amazon and Ebay. Max Media Group was founded by internet visionary Max Cannon and a group of 6 talented University of Washington college students experimenting with some strange new languages called JavaScript and PHP, blending them with an immature markup language called HTML to create e-commerce websites and custom shopping carts. CSS, JQuery and CMS websites would not be created for many years to come. Interestingly, organic Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and paid Search Engine Marketing (SEM), although in their infancy, were alive and kicking in the mid-1990s.


The 'WHY' behind the reason Max Media Group came into existence was an unflinching dedication to the belief that e-commerce would someday take over the world creating an online marketplace in which anyone in the world could purchase virtually any consumer product online and, literally have it delivered to their doorstep. To make a point, in 1997, Max purchased a car online, site unseen, and had it delivered to his house and via his popular e-cmmerce website, shipped beauty supply products to the far reaches of the world. Max's websites have been featured within articles in dozens of international magazines, including InStyle, Vogue, Cosmpolitan and Health magazines. Well, to all the nay-sayers way back then, we now humbly bow and with grace...smile.

Jazz joined the Max Media Group team in 2008, bringing with her a wealth of digital marketing experience, knowledge and skill sets from the non-profit sector. Max and Jazz married, eventually settling in Orlando, Florida to be closer to family, and once they took the seed of Max Media Group and replanted it in central Florida, it began to to grow strong, with deep roots and thrive like the native St. Augustine grass. Jazz and Max are dedicated to building better brands online by harnessing the power of each brand’s individuality and unique “why.”

Max Media Group offers a wide variety of services including website design, software development, digital/print branded graphic design, SEO/SEM strategies, social media marketing, content marketing and email automated marketing - we are extremely passionate about partnering with businesses, assisting them to succeed and grow in the online marketplace.
