Branded Cinemagraphic Display Advertising.

Question?

How do you cut through the the display advertising noise to get your promotional ads noticed, when, in fact, studies show most search and social media users have become 'Ad-Blind' to static marketing images and video banner ads?

Answer.

You gain click-through and conversion traction by juxtapositioning a static image images laid over video images to create eye-catching 'CINEMAGRAPHS'.

It's the contrast of "still vs. moving" components that capture the user's eye, much like an insect wandering across a still surface in your outer peripheral vision - it literally startles you subconsciously into paying attention to the display advertisement.

It's the "something is not as it should be, but, in fact it is" syndrome that gets your ads noticed by delighting your target audience with the unusual.

Solution.

Super simple...let Max Media Group's expert digital graphic artists and content copywriters create visually-compelling, 'CINEMAPGRAPH' digital display ads for your search and social media targeted market campaigns.
